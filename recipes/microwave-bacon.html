Microwave Bacon

Description

What's better than bacon? Precooked, ready-in-a-minute bacon!

Ingredients

  • Precooked microwave bacon

Steps

  1. Buy the bacon. (Hint: Wal-Mart usually has a good selection.)
  2. Open the box and inner plastic bag.
  3. Place 1/2 of the bacon on a paper plate. (Note: You may need to double the paper plates, depending on their thickness.)
  4. Place the paper plate with bacon in the microwave. If possible, use a plastic microwave plate cover to contain the grease splatter. (You can get these covers at Ikea for just a few dollars.)
  5. Cook on high for 1 minute.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5 for the other 1/2 of the bacon.
  7. Let the cooked bacon stand for a minute or two to cool down before eating.
