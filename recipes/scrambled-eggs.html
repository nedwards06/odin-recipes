Scrambled Eggs

Description

The perfect breakfast option for busy people, scrambled eggs are a must-have in your cooking arsenal. Once you learn the basics, you can improve them in a million ways.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Cooking spray
  • Salt (optional)
  • Additional items as desired

Steps

  1. Buy the ingredients.
  2. Warm a pan or skillet on the stove top.
  3. Coat the pan or skillet with cooking spray.
  4. Crack the eggs into a mixing bowl.
  5. Add a small amount of water and salt (if desired), along with any other items you wish to include.
  6. Stir the egg mixture briskly to get some air in.
  7. Pour the mixture into the pan or skillet, and stir/flip repeatedly until cooked.
