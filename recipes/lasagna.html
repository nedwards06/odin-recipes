Lasagna

Description

Lasanga is a classic Italian dish made with flat sheets of pasta, meat sauce, and ricotta cheese. Some variations include other ingredients, such as fresh herbs and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Lasanga noodles
  • Meat sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Fresh herbs and/or vegetables as desired

Steps

  1. Buy the ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven.
  3. Combine ingredients in the proper order, making layers of pasta goodness.
  4. Bake in the oven until done.
  5. Eat.
